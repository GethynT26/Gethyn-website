Plastic pollution

Why we need to stop plastic pollution

Plastic pollution has started to become the most pressing environmental issue, one of the main reasons is that over the decades, we have been increasing plastic generation but not recycling all of that. One of the main reasons why we have increased plastic is because of demand from countries whose population has increased. Also, in some countries plastic pollution is visible because of their garbage collection systems are either inefficient or nonexistent. Also when plastic ends up in the ocean, it can entangle and kill marine life, such as birds, fish, and turtles. It can also be ingested by marine life, which can harm or kill them.

How did this happen

There was a record of a incident with plastic pollution in 1930. scientists were fishing for Plankton and their machine produced lots of plastic litter by accident. But creating plastic also has saved us, as plastic has created medicine with life-saving technology, saved fuel and has saved lives with equipment like helmets. But with this it came with a cost of damaging our planet and environment because of how we dispose of it.There are several sources of plastic pollution, including single-use plastic items such as straws, bags, and water bottles, as well as larger plastic items like fishing nets and shipping containers. Plastic pollution can also come from the breakdown of larger plastic products over time, releasing small plastic particles into the environment.

How does this affect us as the public

This affects our everyday life, as if you went to the beach there will be plastic, and the plastic is dangerous to children as it can cut open their feet or they might eat the plastic. This also affects us as we are slowly consuming micro plastic. Did you know that an average person consumes 70,000 plastic a year. Micro plastics entering the human body through direct exposure to plastic pollution may also cause other deadly issues such as oxidative stress, apoptosis, and necrosis. Plastic pollution in oceans impacts the human health too.

How we can help

There are several ways that we can help reduce plastic pollution, including: Reducing the use of single-use plastics such as straws, water bottles, and plastic bags Recycling plastic products properly Educate others about the issue of plastic pollution and the importance of reducing plastic use. Supporting businesses that have environmentally-friendly practices Using reusable containers and bags for shopping and storage Support businesses that use sustainable packaging materials. By taking these steps, we can help protect the environment and reduce the negative impacts of plastic pollution on wildlife and ecosystems. recycle plastic when possible
